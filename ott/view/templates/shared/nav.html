      Where to buy fares

      • TriMet Tickets app

        Pay your fare instantly with your smartphone.

      • Grocery stores

        Buy fares at Albertsons, Fred Meyer, Safeway and more.

      • Ticket vending machines

        Get tickets from the machines at MAX, WES and Streetcar stations.

      • On the bus

        You can buy a 2½-Hour Ticket or a 1-Day Pass from your bus driver.

      • Online for delivery by mail

        For a small handling fee, you can get tickets and passes by mail.

      • Ticket Office at Pioneer Square

        Open Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.
